== Description ==

An easy way to place a icontact forms on your Wordpress site.

###Quick Start Guide

* [icontact forms quick start guide at thinklandingpages.com](http://www.thinklandingpages.com/icontact-forms/)

###What you get when you use the icontact forms

*  Place a icontact forms with a simple shortcode [icontact_forms id="%postId%"]
*  Put icontact forms on any post or page.
*  icontact forms headline
*  icontact forms message
*  icontact forms button
*  Unlimited icontact forms

###Add icontact forms to any post or page

Whether you are writing a post about your company or another company, you can include a well formatted icontact forms.

###Unlimited icontact forms

Add as many icontact forms to your site as you like.  The icontact forms lets you create forms and save them for when ever you are ready to use them.


== Installation ==

1. Upload icontact-forms to the /wp-content/forms-for-icontact/ directory
1. Activate the icontact forms through the 'iContact Forms' menu in WordPress
1. Click **iContact Forms** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  The icontact forms does the programming and design.

= Is there a limit to the number of icontact forms I can create? =
You can create an unlimited number of GetResponse forms.

= Can the icontact forms be put on my homepage? =
Yes, you can put icontact forms on your homepage by using the shortcode on you homepage.

= Can I put icontact forms on any post or page? =
Yes, use the icontact forms shortcode, [icontact_forms id="%postId%"] on your pages and posts.
